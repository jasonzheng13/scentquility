the benefits of scent therapy

scent therapy offers a wide range of benefits across physical, mental, and emotional wellness.

some of our aromas

heh. just sniff it

jasmine

Inhaling jasmine lifts mood, reduces anxiety, and boosts alertness. It's also widely associated with boosting confidence and romantic feelings. Great for daytime use or creative work.

lavender

Lavender's active compound has been shown to reduce anxiety, lower heart rate, and ease the mind into restful sleep.

sandalwood

Sandalwood is deeply grounding and calming. When inhaled it slows the mind, reduces anxiety, and promotes a meditative, focused stillness.

chamomile

Chamomile is a medicinal herb widely used to promote relaxation, improve sleep quality, and soothe digestive issues, acting as a gentle sedative and anti-inflammatory agent.
