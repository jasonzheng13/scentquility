what is aromatherapy?

about us

Aromatherapy is the practice of using natural plant extracts — particularly essential oils — to support physical and emotional wellbeing. When you inhale scent molecules, they travel directly to the brain's limbic system, the emotional command center that governs mood, memory, and stress response.
